Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Lumen Content Agent is a locally operated content automation tool managed by Proyecto Lumen IA. This policy explains how data is processed when the owner connects an authorized TikTok account.

1. Data we process

With the account owner's explicit authorization, the application may process basic TikTok profile information, account identifiers, publishing permissions, uploaded video information, public engagement metrics, and OAuth access and refresh tokens.

2. How data is used

Data is used only to authenticate the authorized account, upload original videos requested by the account owner, check publishing status, read performance metrics, prevent duplicate actions, and improve the owner's content strategy.

3. Storage and security

Credentials and operational records are stored locally on the account owner's computer. The application does not intentionally transmit TikTok credentials to advertising networks or data brokers. Access is limited to the owner-controlled installation.

4. Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Data is shared only with TikTok as required to provide TikTok functionality and with infrastructure providers strictly when necessary to operate features requested by the owner.

5. Retention and deletion

Tokens and records are retained only while the integration is active. The owner may revoke TikTok access from TikTok account settings and delete the application's local token files at any time.

6. User control

The application acts only on accounts that have completed TikTok's OAuth authorization flow. The owner controls publication privacy and may revoke access at any time.

7. Children's privacy

Lumen Content Agent is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal data.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated as the project evolves. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.
